Privacy Policy

Summy Garden Studio · last updated July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Summy Garden Studio you agree to this policy.

1. Photos & biometric-related data

When you upload or capture a selfie, it is processed to generate your professional headshots. Face photos may be considered biometric-related data. We process your photo only to create the headshots you request. We never sell your photos, and we do not use your photos to train AI models.

2. AI processing

Generation is performed via a third-party AI image service (Google's Gemini API). Your selfie is sent securely to that service solely to produce your headshots and is not retained by us for training.

3. Data retention & deletion

Generated headshots are stored in your account so you can access them from your dashboard. You can request deletion of your account and associated images at any time by contacting support. Source selfies are not permanently stored on our servers.

4. Account data

We store your name, email, credit balance, referral code and generation history to operate your account. Authentication and data storage are provided by Supabase. Passwords are stored only as secure hashes.

5. Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details; Stripe processes them under its own security standards.

6. Cookies

Essential cookies keep you signed in and track your credits — the service cannot work without them. Optional analytics cookies help us understand usage and are only set if you click "Accept all". You can choose "Essential only" at any time.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location (including UK GDPR and Hong Kong PDPO), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, and to withdraw consent. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Contact

Questions or requests: support@summygarden.studio.
